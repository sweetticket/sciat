CONSENT FORM

Purpose of this measure: The purpose of this study is to gather information about people’s cognitive reactions to information related to importance and the Spartan Race. What you will do: Complete a task where you have to sort stimuli into categories as quickly as you can. We will also compare your reactions on this task with your performance and involvement in Spartan races. Time required: The study will take approximately 5 minutes to complete.

Risks: There is no more risk than would be expected from typical everyday experiences.

Benefits: There are no direct benefits associated with participating.

Compensation: You will not receive any compensation for this study. Your participation in this study is voluntary. You may refuse to participate before the study begins or discontinue at any time, with no penalty to you, and no effect on your relationship with either Cornell University or Spartan.com.

Confidentiality: All survey data you provide will be strictly confidential. However please note that because the internet connection for completing the survey is not encrypted, there is always the chance that the survey data you provide could be intercepted by a third party.

Participation and withdrawal: Your participation in this survey is completely voluntary, and you may quit at any time by simply closing your web browser.

Contact: If you have questions about this study, please contact Melissa Ferguson at mjf44@cornell.edu. You may also report your concerns or complaints anonymously through Ethicspoint’s website (www.ethicspoint.com) or by calling toll free at 1-866-293-3077. Ethicspoint is an independent organization that serves as a liaison between the University and the person bringing the complaint so that anonymity can be ensured.
