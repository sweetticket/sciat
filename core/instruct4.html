For each word, you will use the “z” (LEFT) and “.” (RIGHT) keys.

There will be two halves to this portion of the experiment. In the first half, you will use the LEFT key to categorize words related to how you successfully complete a Spartan race and synonyms of the word important. You will use the RIGHT key to categorize words that are synonyms of the word unimportant.



LEFT (z) = Spartan-How OR Important

RIGHT (.) = Unimportant



You will first complete a practice block to make sure you have the task down.

If you are incorrect on a trial, the word "INCORRECT" will appear on the screen for a few seconds before moving on to the next word. Just wait till the next word comes up.

If you take too long to answer a question, the phrase "TOO SLOW" will appear, reminding you that on future trials, you have to respond within 1.5 seconds.

To indicate you are ready to start the practice trials, press 'Start'.
